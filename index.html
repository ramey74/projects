Ooltewah

OHS Website

Ooltewah is in the Eastern part of Chattanooga. Ooltewah has the largest high school in the Chattanooga area. Their mascot are the Owls.

Red Bank

RBHS Website

Red Bank is in the Northwest side of Chattanooga. It is home to Red Bank High School.Red Bank Tigers are their mascot.

East Ridge

ERHS Website

East Ridge is the Southern most city in Chattanooga. It is home to the East Ridge Pioneers.

Brainerd

BHS Website

Brainerd is just north of East Ridge. It is home to the Brainerd Bills of Brainerd High School.
